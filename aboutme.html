Hello Everone!

Welcome to my about me page! Here I will talk a little about myself, my future goals, and my thoughts on this semester.

Me

I am currently studying Design and Technology with a minor in film production at Parsons School of Design in New York City. I have always been passionate about film production and just recently found interest in coding. I grew up in Annapolis, Maryland and loved it there, but have always loved New York City and knew I wanted to go to college there. In my free time I enjoy being social, going to concerts, watching movies and being around my friends.

My Future

My goal is to work in the film industry in the future. I enjoy making short films as well as analyzing films, including their music, cinematography, and storyline. I do enjoy design and technology, and I believe that understanding these topics can be helpful for the film industry, especially when it comes to the backstage production of some technological elements. One day, I would love to work as a producer and produce my own movie.

Reflection on this semester

Over the past few months, my relationship with technology has definitely improved. I've discovered a lot of material that I had never heard of, and this has me motivated to study more in the future. Many of these assignments have been difficult, but once I realized what I needed to do, it was really fascinating and fun to see it play out. This semester was very exciting, and I'm looking forward to what lies ahead.
